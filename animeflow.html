Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the AnimeFlow. Find answers to the most common questions below.

What is AnimeFlow?

Anime Flow is a seasonal anime rating and tracking app built to stop review bombing. Instead of rating a whole show at once, users give ratings to individual episodes (1–10 stars). The show’s overall score is the average of its episode ratings, ensuring fairer results.

Why not just rate the whole show at once?

Rating by episode prevents inflated or unfair scores caused by review bombing, early hype, or disappointment in the final arc. It ensures the rating is balanced across the entire season.

Does Anime Flow have community features?

Yes! Till now, the Anime Flow community has grown to over 350K digital followers across platforms.

When will Anime Flow be available?

The app is currently in development. You can sign up for early access to be among the first to try it.
